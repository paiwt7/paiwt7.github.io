Using Paint Brush


Using a paint brush is an excellent way to create new designs. No Paiwt Expander is needed, but when used it can enhance. Here I am painting on a piece of wax paper.



Once you have finished spreading Paiwt Medium and dropping your Paiwt Colors, use your paint brush to mix the colors and create your unique design.
