Unique Paiwt Creatures

A Unique Paiwt Creature (UPC) Enjin ERC-1155 NFT Token is the result of a Unique Paiwt Explosion’s (UPE) ERC-1155 metamorphosis. Every UPE is capable of going through a metamorphosis, which creates a new separate UPC, but only happens at random and is an extremely rare occurrence. The result is two ERC-1155 NFT Tokens, both a UPE and a UPC.

Once a UPC has completed its metamorphosis it will have a:

  • distinct visual appearance.
  • unique name.
  • Enjin amount of 10.
  • maximum total supply of 1.
