Compete in a board game using paint to complete unique artwork that enables you to join an interactive blockchain market.

Paiwt [PAWNT] is a board game where your creations create Enjin backed ERC-1155 Tokens. Players create Unique Paiwt Explosions (or UPE for short) according to the game rules and then be submitted for minting. Individual Unique Paiwt Explosions will have a distinct visual appearance determined by the sequence of colors players choose during the game. After Unique Paiwt Explosions become minted into an offical Paiwt UPE ERC-1155 Token, they can be bought, sold, or transferred digitally. All the block digital Unique Paiwt Explosions are unique and 100% owned and controlled by the artists; they cannot be replicated, stolen, or destroyed.
