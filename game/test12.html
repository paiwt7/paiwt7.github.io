UPE NFT Mint Shop

All tokens minted through this service are Enjin ERC-1155 Blockchain Assets that are cross-platform compatible, automatically supported by the Enjin Wallet, powered by a robust suite of modular smart contracts—and therefore more advanced than Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Enjin Coin itself. To get started, you must first own the official Paiwt The Board Game. Playing the game allows you to create your Unique Paiwt Explosions. Once you have completed a game of Paiwt, and your designs are dry, you are able to scan them in. After you have your scanned picture, you will need to send it to us unedited. Once recieved, we will make sure everything is cleaned up and proportionalized for minting. Minting can take up to 10 Business Days. Once minted, your Official Paiwt UPE ERC-1155 Token will be sent your Enjin Wallet. Official Paiwt UPE ERC-1155 Tokens will be backed by 5 Enjin.
