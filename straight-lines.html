Straight Lines

Straight Lines

This new technique creates straight lines, checkerboards, and new designs. You will need: a plastic card, a surface to paint on (here I am using a piece of cardboard with a plastic layer over the top), a "palet" to paint on (here I am using a shorter piece of cardboard with a plastic layer over the top), Paiwt Medium, and an Paiwt Color. Start by pouring Paiwt Medium into the center. Next, do a straight line of Paiwt Color onto your pallet.


Dip your plastic card onto your palet color, covering the bottom of your card.

Now place your color covered card onto your Paiwt Medium.




Spectacular! You have made a new straight design using a plastic card!
