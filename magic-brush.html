Magic Brush


This technique is fun and colorful, I call it "Magic Brush". To create your magic brush, mix equal parts of Paiwt Expander with Paiwt Colors then dip your brush into the mixture.



Create new unique designs with your magic brush using different Paiwt Colors mixed with Paiwt Expander.




You can make new stunning artwork with your "Magic Brush".
