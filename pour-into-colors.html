Pour into Colors




Pouring Paiwt Medium into the center of your Paiwt Colors creates new designs. No Paiwt Expander is needed. Here I am painting on a plastic grocery bag.





Once you have finished spreading Paiwt Medium and dropping your Paiwt Colors, pour Paiwt Medium into the middle of your Paiwt Colors. Try to aim for the center.





Wonderful! A great looking unique design using Paiwt Medium in the center!
