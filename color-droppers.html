Paiwt Color Droppers

Paiwt Color Droppers offer an art form that is unique, when used with Paiwt Medium they produce an organic effect. The process is easy, safe, and fun.


There are 9 different Paiwt Colors including: Red, Yellow, Purple, Green, Orange, Blue, Brown, Pink, and Black.

When two or more Paiwt Colors are dropped onto or around each other close enough to combine, the colors will form a bond that grows larger together. New colors and designs can be achieved by then using Paiwt Expander.
