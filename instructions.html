Instructions

To get started you will need: Paiwt White Medium, Paiwt Color Droppers, Paiwt Expander, and a flat level surface to paint on.


Lightly shake or stir Paiwt Medium, then pour out and spread desired amount onto a flat level surface, like canvas.


Quickly squeeze out Paiwt Colors onto fresh wet Paiwt Medium. Drop two or more different colors on top or touching each other.


Drop Paiwt Expander onto your Paiwt Colors.


Visit our Techniques page for new ideas.
