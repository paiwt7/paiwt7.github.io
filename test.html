About

Paiwt is a unique all-natural and non-toxic painting technique sold in a one of a kind art kit that produces permanent abstract organic dendrite fractals using a two step method, pour then drop!

Products

Colorful dendrite fractal painting supplies and artwork.  Learn about Paiwt products including White Medium, Color Droppers, and Expander. Visit our Amazon Store for genuine Paiwt Supplies.

Instructions

Follow Step-By-Step instructions on exactly how to use Paiwt. Learn what materials are needed, along with a basic technique to get you started. For more advanced ways of using Paiwt, visit our Techniques page.
